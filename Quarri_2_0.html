Server-side scripts, automated action cascades and massive-scale data — plus a fully redesigned interface. Quarri 2.0 is here.
 
In this release
A bigger, more capable Quarri — on the same secure core.
2.0 adds server-side scripts, automated action cascades and large-scale data exchange on top of the deterministic, permissioned foundation your team already relies on — plus a fully redesigned workspace. We ship improvements continuously; below are the highlights.
● Server-side scripts   ● Action cascades   ● Data at scale   ● Redesigned interface
 
 
What’s new in 2.0
01 · Your logic, server-side
Write a script, run it on Quarri
Custom transformations, one-off clean-ups, recurring jobs — write a script and run it on Quarri’s own server, on demand or on a schedule. Your team’s logic now lives next to your data instead of on someone’s laptop, versioned and repeatable.
 
 
02 · Automation
Chain actions into ordered cascades
Sequence steps into a cascade that runs in order — load the data, transform it, refresh a metric, publish a dashboard, alert the team — automatically, in the right order, every time. Set it up once and let it run on its own.
 
 
03 · Large data exchange
Move massive datasets in and out
Bulk-load big files straight into the Quarri server and pull large extracts back out. Work with massive datasets without overwhelming your context window, chunking or workarounds, or blowing up token usage on scripts — ingested in one pass and kept query-ready.
 
 
The workspace, rebuilt
Redesigned interface
Full-screen dashboards
Dashboards no longer open in a cramped preview — they fill the whole screen. Open any dashboard edge-to-edge, or go fullscreen for a clean, presentation-ready view with the menus tucked away.
 
 
Sharing
Scheduled sharing
Send a share link to grant access to the live dashboard, or use distribution to email a static copy to any address or saved list — once, or on a schedule. Recipients don’t need a Quarri login.
 
 
Insights
See how your team uses Quarri
The new Engagement view shows query volume, live dashboards, your busiest hours and a top-10 leaderboard of your most active users — plus the skills answering your team’s questions.
 
 
Admin
Your plan, seats & limits — at a glance
Plan & Limits shows exactly what you’re using and what’s left. Builder seats are capped to your plan; view-only users are unlimited, so share with the whole company without counting seats.
 
 
Live data
Always in sync — and you can see it
Every connected source shows when it last synced and when it’s due to sync next, so you always know your dashboards are reading the latest. Need fresh numbers now? Hit Sync now to refresh on demand.
