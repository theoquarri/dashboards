The Challenge
Multi-channel apparel is data-rich but insight-poor
Shopify captures DTC. EDI captures wholesale. Monday.com captures production. But no single view connects channel margins, inventory positions, and partner sell-through — leaving revenue and margin on the table.

Channel data lives in silos

DTC revenue in Shopify, wholesale in EDI/spreadsheets, production costs in Monday.com — comparing channel profitability means manual wrangling across systems every time.

Sell-through visibility comes too late

Wholesale partner performance — sell-through rates, markdown exposure, return rates — is often known after the damage is done, not in time to adjust assortments or shift inventory.

Launches depend on tribal knowledge

Drop readiness, wholesale shipping windows, and production timelines live in Monday.com, Shopify, and people's heads — no single view of what's on track vs at risk across brands.
